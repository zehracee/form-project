Evaluation of Titles Predicted by LLM Using Abstract Information

This will be used to measure the success of LLM models in generating titles based on abstracts. There are four different options created by four distinct models, based on the Turkish and English abstracts you will read. We kindly ask you to evaluate these options in terms of clarity, fluency, relevance, and consistency. The evaluation scale ranges from 1 to 5, with 1 being the lowest and 5 being the highest score. We thank you for sharing your contributions and valuable experiences with us.
